    • What is AetherScore?

    AetherScore is a software toolkit, for creating & delivering flexible and dynamic cues or prompts, in real-time, on the fly, to individual performers through a precisely networked multi-screen interface.



    Who is AetherScore for?

    AetherScore is for improvisers, composers, experimentalists, conductors, performers, multimedia artists, educators, students, researchers, dolphins, unicorns, and of course, kittens. So if you identify as any of those things, it's for YOU.



    What does AetherScore do?

    It delivers real time text scores, animated graphic notation, reactive video score, visual metronome syncronization, very large ensembles of over 100 people if needed. The possibilities are marvelous.
